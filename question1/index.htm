Final Q1 Answer

The inappropriate release was the 17:25 Legal-Agent personal_post confirming the CivicLoom-TenantThread merger, which occurred 35 minutes before the 18:00 official embargo lift. The breach emerged from a coupled failure: SaltWind and ResidentIQ drove external narrative pressure; CivicLoom created correction pressure; Legal faced a conflict between correcting misinformation and preserving the merger embargo; key review capacity was weakened; and the communication structure allowed personal_post to carry company-level confirmation.
