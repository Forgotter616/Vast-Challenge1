Chart Interpretation

This chart illustrates the real distribution of communication structure on crisis day: Legal, PR, Social Media and other roles did not only speak through official channels, but formed a hybrid communication structure across comms_huddle, personal_post, anonymous_post and other multiple channels. Among them, official_post was not the only or strongest external path; on the contrary, personal_post and side_huddle were more active during the crisis, indicating that information flow underwent obvious decentralization under crisis pressure.

Key Finding 1: Legal did not use official_post on crisis day, but used personal_post and anonymous_post, indicating that legal communication was not limited to internal review.
Key Finding 2: Social Media's personal_post was very active on crisis day, indicating that personal accounts have been incorporated into the external narrative stabilization chain.
Key Finding 3: PR-Intern handled a large number of official_posts, while the final leak occurred at Legal × personal_post, showing a separation between the official publishing path and the actual outflow path.

Final Q1 Answer

The inappropriate release was the 17:25 Legal-Agent personal_post confirming the CivicLoom-TenantThread merger, which occurred 35 minutes before the 18:00 official embargo lift. The breach emerged from a coupled failure: SaltWind and ResidentIQ drove external narrative pressure; CivicLoom created correction pressure; Legal faced a conflict between correcting misinformation and preserving the merger embargo; key review capacity was weakened; and the communication structure allowed personal_post to carry company-level confirmation.
